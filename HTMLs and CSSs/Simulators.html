YMCA's use of Simulators in education

Zareh Demirdji, an author and science teacher at YMCA Academy, he wanted to teach the students about the science makers, since he read many backstories of those scientist throughout the history, later on he came to a realization that 90% of the students were actually gamers, so here the idea of using videogames for education came and he decided to use a game called “Sidmeiers Civilization” which is a turn-based 4x game (4x stands for explore, exploit, expand and exterminate), the purpose is linking this game to the course of history and geography
