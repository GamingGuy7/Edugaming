The Message of Persona 5

The Youtuber "Aleczandxr" is one the best youtuber you could find in terms of discussing the messages, philosophies, psychologies , ideals, morality of character and more. usually he handles Japanese anime but this time around he picks up Persona 5 due to the game's rich story and ideology, this video is meant for those who finished the game "Persona 5" and wish to learn more about the depth of its story's philosophy and themes. Games like these are good examples of stories that could keep you thinking about the world and our lives in a sense of wonder, also his youtube channel is recommended if you are interested in learning this youtuber's perspective and theories over different animes and games.




















The Story of Godot

Ace Attorney is one of the most known visual novels out there, its entry "Ace attorney Trials & Tribulations" is considered by many of the series fans to be the best game in the series, i believe one of the major reasons is the introducing of the character "Godot" who made such a strong and well reserved presence in this game creating a long term legacy for AAT&T's story. In this video the youtuber "TheStoryteller" will discuss his observation of Godot's character and role in the game, his mentality, personality, the reasons he did what he did and how he is a human just like any other who acknowledges his flaws which were caused by the tragic events throughout his life, and of course this video is meant to those who already finished the game.

Persona 4 saved my life

Have you ever wondered if Videogames stories could actually be that much referrable to real life that it could change someone's life to the better? here is an interesting story of a person who had the courage to share about his personal suffering story and how playing through the game "Persona 4 Golden" made him learn from the game's themes and lessons, as the game is discussing human's abilities to better themselves by accepting their true self including their personal flaws as humans and carry on with life while having faith that someday they should find a place where they belong amongst people and society.
