E-sports

Electronic sports or E-sports are a form of competition using Videogames. To alot of people in the general public Videogames are considered as a hobby to spend time on for refreashment after a tough day, having fun, or even waste time, but to some others (The E-sports players) it is a carrier or a sport that require actual practice to gain skills and get better just like how carriers such as magicians or normal sports players practice. here we are going to discuss about various genres and aspects such as Rhythm, Fighting, First person shooter , Real-time strategy and more, discussing the nature of those games and how E-sports games can potentially improve your critical thinking, reaction times , ability to form strategies, understanding the opponent's mentality and psychology and more all from the E-sports point of view. Life Teaching Stories

Many games feature a story to keep the player engaged throughout the game, but in some Videogames the story author or creator tries to deliver a message or a theory to the player through the game's story, whether that story is based on real life stories, personal experiences or imaginary stories, we can find these stories so much relatable to our actualy lives. Stories can be delivered in many ways... through Novels, visual comics, Movies, Animated cartoons and more, but the difference in experiencing a story through a videogame is that you get to interact with the envirounment of the game, as a result you gain more attachment to the characterts the story. Videogames is a medium that combines different types of arts into an enjoyable and interactive experience. here we are going to discuss about different slice of life stories of some good videogames we recommend to players looking for a game with relatable stories and characters.
